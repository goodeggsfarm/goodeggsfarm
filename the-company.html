ABOUT US

Good Eggs Farm was founded in 2024 by IT students, a passionate entrepreneur with a vision to create a sustainable and ethical egg production company. After years of research and planning, IT students decided to start Good Eggs Farm with the goal of providing fresh, high-quality eggs to customers while promoting sustainable and ethical farming practices. The company started with a small flock of chickens and has since grown to become one of the leading egg production companies in the region. Today, Good Eggs Farm is known for its commitment to animal welfare, environmental sustainability, and customer satisfaction. We are proud to be a leader in the egg production industry and look forward to continuing to provide our customers with the best eggs possible.

Good Egg Farm is committed to delivering exceptional service and compliant
screening services that help companies hire more good eggs, faster.
